Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026 · Last updated: July 3, 2026

HealDiet ("the App", "we", "us") helps people with chronic conditions manage their diet. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why, who we share it with, and the choices and rights you have. HealDiet is operated by Heung Gi Kim, an individual developer, who is the data controller. Questions? Contact khgsyk@gmail.com.

Not medical advice. HealDiet provides general nutrition and wellness information and is not a medical device or a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making decisions about your health, diet, or medication.

2.How we use your information

  • Provide core features: log foods, estimate nutrition, show condition-aware recommendations, track biomarkers and progress.
  • Analyze food/fridge photos you submit to identify foods and estimate nutrition.
  • Personalize targets and meal suggestions to your conditions and goals.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, fix bugs, and improve the App.
  • Respond to your requests and provide support.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your health data for advertising.

3.Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

  • Consent — for health data, photos, and importing Apple Health data. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Contract — to provide the App you request.
  • Legitimate interests — security, preventing abuse, and improving the App.
  • Legal obligation — where we must retain or disclose data by law.

5.Apple Health (HealthKit)

  • We access Apple Health data only after you grant permission, and only the types needed (e.g., body weight).
  • HealthKit data is never used for advertising or marketing, is not sold, and is not shared with third parties for their own purposes.
  • You can revoke access anytime in the iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.

6.Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. You can clear your logged data in the App ("reset my data"), or ask us to delete your account and associated data by emailing khgsyk@gmail.com. We will delete or anonymize data within a reasonable period unless we must keep it to comply with the law.

7.International transfers

We are based in the United States, and our providers (including Supabase and OpenAI) process data in the United States. If you use the App from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. and other countries where our providers operate.

8.Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live (e.g., EEA/UK under GDPR, or California under CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information;
  • Export a copy of your data (portability);
  • Withdraw consent or object to certain processing;
  • Not receive discrimination for exercising your rights.

We do not sell or "share" personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA. To exercise any right, email khgsyk@gmail.com. You may also manage or delete much of your data directly in the App.

9.Security

We protect your data using encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and row-level security on our database. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information and limit access to it.

10.Children's privacy

HealDiet is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

11.Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will change the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the App or by other reasonable means. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
